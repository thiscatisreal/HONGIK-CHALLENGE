WE MADE

Image Attendance Authentication System with Deep Learning

The "Image Attendance Authentication System with Deep Learning" is a system that applies image classification technology, recognizes and classifies a person's face and automatically checks attendance.
Through this system, attendance management is possible not only during the start of the class, but also during the class. Therefore, it prevents cheating in which you leave in the middle or other students attend instead.
In addition, we provide an easy-to-use website for each student so that both students and professors can conveniently check attendance.

IDEA

We felt that attendance checks were spent a lot of time taking classes. If the number of students is large, it takes 10 minutes to check attendance. Spending a lot of time before starting classes is not only a problem for students but also for professors.
The existing electronic attendance system can shorten the attendance check time, but the location tracking to prevent proxy attendance was sufficient for other students in class to log in with the ID of a friend who was not in class and attend on their behalf.
Also, there are many students who attend and sneak out of the lecture. This obscures the atmosphere of the class and makes it difficult to admit legitimate attendance. We came up with this project to eliminate these inconveniences.
